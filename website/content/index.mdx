# smbCloud

**Four services. One platform.** Enterprise power at startup velocity.

smbCloud is the platform you build your product on. It bundles the pieces most
teams stitch together by hand, meaning email your users actually receive, sign-in
you don't have to build, app hosting, and a database, into one place, so you can
stay focused on what you're actually building.
